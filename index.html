Global Warming and Its Many Benefits

I'm sure that most of you know what global warming is. In simple terms, it is the slow steady rise in Earth's surface temperature. What most of you don't know is that global warming is not bad at all. Recently, scientists and politicians have been fearmongering the citizens of the world with the supposed threats that global warming brings or will bring. Well, these people are twisting the facts to benefit themselves. The 'threats' of global warming will actually make our planet much better! One effect of global warming that scientists are claiming will destroy our planet is the melting of the ice caps. When the ice caps melt, the sea level will rise dramatically. A lot of animals will die, but it's not our fault that they didn't just get away from the water. The shore will move farther inland which means that everyone is now closer to the beach, and who doesn't love the beach? Global warming will also make the average temperature go up. That means that we won't have to pay money for heat in our homes, saving us a lot of money. Thousands more people will die from heat-related problems every year, but who cares when we're saving money! Use that extra money to go see a doctor! If you're too hot just turn your AC on. With global warming, millions of animals will eventually die, which is obviously a good thing. Many of the animals that will die are probably dangerous which will make our world safer to live in, and it will leave more fruits and other food left for us to consume.

Some Downsides to Global Warming

Although global warming is pretty much perfect, there are a few small things about it that aren't too great. Take a look:

  • More and Stronger Hurricanes
  • Increased Temperatures
  • More Acidic Oceans
  • Species Going Extinct
  • Increased Death Rates

As you can see, there are several negative effects of global warming. The intensity, frequency, and duration of hurricanes will become longer greater. The planet will get hotter and hotter each year and the oceans will become more acidic from absorbing our excess emissions. Possibly hundreds of species of animals could go extinct because of the increased heat and their ecosystems being destroyed, and more humans will also die because of the heat. Despite these things, the good obviously outweighs the bad. Saving money, having closer beaches, and having more food is definitely a good tradeoff, right?
